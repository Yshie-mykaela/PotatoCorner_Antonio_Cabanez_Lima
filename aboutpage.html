    About Us


    Potato Corner is a global food franchise known for its flavored French fries. The company was founded in the Philippines in 1992 by Jose Magsaysay Jr., Ricky Montelibano, Danny Bermejo, and George Wieneke. Potato Corner's first stall opened up in SM Megamall in Quezon City, and the company quickly became a popular snack food destination in the Philippines.


    Potato Corner is a popular choice for people of all ages. The company's bright green stalls are a common sight in malls, markets, and other public places. Potato Corner is also a popular choice for parties and events.

    HISTORY

    Potato Corner was founded in 1992 by Jose Magsaysay Jr., Ricky Montelibano, Danny Bermejo, and George Wieneke. The company was originally called "Fries Corner," but it was later changed to "Potato Corner" to reflect the company's focus on flavored French fries.

    Potato Corner's first stall opened up in SM Megamall in October 1992 in the Philippines. The company quickly became popular, and it began franchising the year after.

    Potato Corner received the Franchise Excellence Hall of Fame Award by the Philippine Franchise Association and Department of Trade and Industry. The company has also won Best Franchise of the Year on three consecutive years.
